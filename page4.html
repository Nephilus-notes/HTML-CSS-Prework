Kraethor

Dralfian's homeland


Since reading Dragonlance and Tolkien as a child I have always made up my own world to put stories in. From creating Dralfian, elves morphed by magic into powerful half dragon hybrids, to how goblins wered forced underground and emerged as short, stunted creatures with black bones and skin that glows with bioluminescence, Kraethor has a rich history I've still barely scratched the surface of.
